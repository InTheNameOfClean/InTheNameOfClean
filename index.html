About Our Company

Who We Are

In the Name of Clean LLC is dedicated to providing top-notch cleaning services across various sectors including post-construction, residential, commercial, federal, and state contracting. We are also expanding our expertise into building management.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional cleaning and management services to our clients, ensuring their spaces are spotless and well-maintained. We strive to exceed expectations and build long-lasting relationships with our customers.

Our Services

Post-Construction Cleaning

We provide comprehensive cleaning services to ensure newly constructed or renovated buildings are immaculate and ready for use.

Residential Cleaning

Our team is experienced in maintaining clean, comfortable homes for our clients, tailored to their specific needs and schedules.

Commercial Cleaning

We offer professional cleaning services for offices, retail spaces, and other commercial properties, ensuring a clean and healthy environment for employees and customers.

Federal & State Contracting

We are equipped to handle large-scale cleaning projects for federal and state buildings, adhering to strict standards and regulations.

Building Management

Our new building management services ensure that all aspects of property maintenance and operations are handled efficiently and effectively.
